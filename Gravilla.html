313 Gravilla St.

DETAILS

313 Gravilla St. was renovated in 2016 and is currently held for long-term lease. The minimum lease term is 12 months. The 3,386 square feet of living area is well designed for todays family living and offers 5 bedrooms and 4 1/2 bathrooms. It is located one block from the most famous surfing beach in Southern California, Windansea beach. The home is fully furnished. It incorporates a classic design with an open floor plan kitchen and family rooms. It has high quality fixtures and appliances throughout out, including Wolf ovens and Sub-Zero refrigerators. The large windows, doors and five skylights bring in a lot of light without sacrificing privacy. The property has a separate apartment and living area that is perfect for older children or guests. Enjoy the fantastic ocean views and sunsets from the two outdoor decks. If you enjoy living in an elegant home near the beach, this is the property for you. Contact Kenneth Riis for availablity and leasing information.
