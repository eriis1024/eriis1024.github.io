ABOUT

BTL Properties was established in 2016 to build, manage and restore luxury residential properties in Southern California. We are a family owned business that utilizes our own design and construction techniques to build properties with an end-user/owner’s mentally. We build properties that we would want to live in. This "Built to Live” mentality really sets us apart. We feel that all design and construction decisions should be based on the long-term viability of the property. As a result, the quality of our projects stand on their own. Each property is built and designed to appeal to high-end luxury buyers with discerning tastes. We feel that well built, classically designed properties using the best materials will always generate the highest property value. Since 2016, we have built or repositioned four properties and have one project, 1781 Colgate Circle in La Jolla California, currently under construction. Given our capitalization, we have the option to either sell or hold for lease a property upon completion. To date, we have utilized both options. Either way, the creation of a property that will stand the test of time is our overriding priority. Please contact Kenneth Riis for updates on our current project under construction or if you have interest in leasing any of our homes.
